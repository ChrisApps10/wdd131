JDM Legends Auto Site Plan

Site Name: JDM Legends Auto

The name "JDM Legends Auto" was chosen to clearly communicate the website's focus on cars from the Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) that are considered classic or iconic. It positions the site as a premier destination for enthusiasts seeking these specific types of vehicles. "Legends" evokes a sense of heritage and high quality, appealing to knowledgeable buyers and enthusiasts.

Site Purpose

The JDM Legends Auto website serves as a digital storefront and informational hub for American enthusiasts of Japanese Domestic Market cars. The site's primary purpose is to showcase the available vehicle inventory, provide detailed information about JDM imports, and facilitate a seamless contact process for potential buyers. It caters to a passionate niche market by presenting high-quality images and descriptions of curated, legendary vehicles.

Scenarios for Contact Us Page

  • Scenario Message 1: "I am looking for a specific JDM model like a Nissan Skyline or a Toyota Supra. How can I see if you have one in stock and what information do you provide?"
  • Scenario Message 2: "I am new to the JDM import process. Can I find out how to contact your sales team to learn more about a car I'm interested in and how purchasing works?"
  • Scenario Message 3: "I want to see what's currently available. Where can I find a list of all your cars?"
